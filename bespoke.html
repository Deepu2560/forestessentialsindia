FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

FAQs


Q: Is this interaction complimentary?
A: This interaction comes with a small fee of INR 500/- which will be redeemable as a coupon when you choose to purchase any of our products on Forest Essentials website within one week of your consult call.

Q: What is the primary mode of interaction?
A: The primary mode will be a telephonic conversation with our Ayurvedic Doctor. Once you have scheduled your call, you will receive a confirmation through email.

Q: How long will the interaction be for?
A: It will be a 15-minute-long telephonic interaction.

Q: What are the available days and timings for the interaction?
A: Depending on the Doctor's availability, telephonic interactions are available throughout the week. Please check the calendar for available slots.

Q: What languages can the interaction be done in?
A: Our Ayurvedic Doctors are fluent in Hindi and English.

Q: Where can I share feedback in case I am dissatisfied with the interaction?
A: To help us serve you better in the future we would love to hear about your experience with our Ayurvedic Doctors. You can write to us at service@forestessentialsindia.com or call us at +91 8010200666.

Q: How can I reschedule my appointment?
A: You will have the option to reschedule your appointment on the confirmation email we send to you after you have scheduled your first appointment.

Q: How do I book myself a follow-up interaction?
A: You can follow the same 3-step process to book yourself a follow-up interaction.

Q: Do I have to purchase the products suggested to me to redeem the coupon?
A: The coupon is redeemable against the purchase of any product listed on the Forest Essentials website.

Q: What do I do in case I face issues while redeeming my coupon?
A: You can write to us at service@forestessentialsindia.com and our support team will resolve your issue at the earliest.

Q: If I return the product I have purchased post my consult, will the voucher value be refunded too?
A: The voucher can be redeemed only once within one week of your consultation call and cannot be refunded in case you choose not to make a purchase. In the event you purchase products and decide to return them, the amount refunded will be the difference after applying the INR 500 voucher. Also, in case you purchase a product of a value lower than INR 500, the difference in the voucher will elapse.
